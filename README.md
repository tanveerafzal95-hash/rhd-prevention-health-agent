# RHD Prevention Health Agent
PHCA8010 Capstone Project
## Problem
Acute Rheumatic Fever (ARF) and Rheumatic Heart Disease (RHD) continue to affect Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander children in remote Australian communities.
## Target Population
Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander children aged 5-14 years and their families.
## Golden Source
Australian Guideline for Prevention, Diagnosis and Management of Acute Rheumatic Fever and Rheumatic Heart Disease (Edition 3.3, August 2025).
## Safety
This tool provides educational information only. It does not diagnose disease, prescribe medication, or replace healthcare professionals.
